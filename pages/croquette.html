Potato Croquettes



Description

You've gotta have em! Don't use too much black pepper though!

    Ingredients

  • 5 lb bag of potatoes
  • 8 oz of Mozzarella
  • ½ cup of Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • ½ lb (or 8 oz) of Salami (Deli meat or sausage)
  • ¼ cup of pepper- cutie thinks still too much, I would halve it again to 2 tbs
  • 1 tbsp of Salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of Italian style bread crumbs
  • 1 tbsp of flour

    Instructions

  1. Peel, boil and mash the potatoes the night before. Refrigerate.

  2. Shred mozzarella, grate parmesan, dice salami.

  3. Combine the flour and breadcrumbs in a small dish. In a separate dish, whisk together 2 eggs.

  4. Combine the Potatoes, the salami, both cheeses, and the salt & pepper thoroughly in a large mixing bowl.

  5. In a large, deep pan, heat ½ an ich of vegetable oil over medium heat

  6. Shape the potatoes into small, hand-sized loaves.

  7. Dip the potato loaves into the eggs, and roll in breadcrumbs to coat evenly.

  8. Carefully fry the loaves on every side until golden-brown. Set on a paper towel lined plate to dry.
